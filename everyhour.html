Every Hour

just as i was inspired by the poets, by the poems, i was motivated by your love. so oh love, my love, take hold of me and never let go, for i am worthless if not to be seen by you, for i am useless if not to be of any use to you. for i want to die in nowhere but your arms, for i can't see a home that isn't by your shore. and i don't know if you love me, but— oh lover, my lover, i love you so much more. my heart gets sore, it pains, it aches, every time it's reminded of you. reminded of you, every hour i am, by everything i do. i miss it with you. so i wonder— everything we do, do you miss it too? like the way i think of you, do you think of me too? i understand, but still, i wish you were here— and a liar i would be if i said otherwise. and i know we will won't meet, but oh dove, my dove, every hour, you kill me, what if i, die tonight?
